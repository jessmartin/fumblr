About mumblr

My name is Jess Martin and this is my mumblr.

Mumblr is a microblog, in the vein of Tumblr. It's a tribute to a simpler time of blogging, a few steps away from the noise and platform paranoia of twitter. It's a quiet place to share my thoughts without distraction.

Strange Software
Also, mumblr is a way for me to learn about decentralized applications. The software that powers this blog, mumblr, is a little bizarre. If you'd like to learn more about it, I recommend you head to the README in GitHub.
